Description

The Mumbai Museum, also known as the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya, is a prominent museum in Mumbai. It is located in the heart of the city and showcases a rich collection of art, artifacts, and cultural exhibits.

The museum is renowned for its historical significance, housing artifacts from various periods, including sculptures, paintings, and decorative arts.
